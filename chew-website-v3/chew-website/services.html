Programs

One system. Three ways to build it with us.

These aren't separate services — they're tiers of the same infrastructure: education, strategy, and implementation accountability. Admission is by application, and every applicant is matched to the tier that fits their readiness.

◆

Infrastructure Program

Who it's for: individuals and entrepreneurs ready to build their financial systems from the ground up.

What's built: education, strategy, and implementation accountability — the core CHEW system.

What's expected of you: participation, honesty, and follow-through on assigned action items.

◆

Executive Advisory

Who it's for: founders and individuals with more complex situations who want premium 1:1 access.

What's built: the Infrastructure Program's foundation, plus direct, higher-touch strategist access.

What's expected of you: the same accountability, at a pace matched to more complex decisions.

◆

Membership

Who it's for: clients who've completed a program and want ongoing support.

What's built: continued access to education, strategy check-ins, and your CHEW advisor.

What's expected of you: staying engaged with your systems, not just maintaining them passively.

What CHEW doesn't do

We won't call it something it isn't.

CHEW does not offer "credit repair," guaranteed loan approvals, or promises of specific financial outcomes. We don't dispute accurate information on a credit report on your behalf, and we don't act as a substitute for a licensed CPA, attorney, or registered investment advisor. Where a need falls into one of those categories, we'll tell you directly and point you to the right kind of professional.
