How It Works

Ten stages. One long-term relationship.

1

Application

You apply for admission and answer honestly — it's how we know whether we can serve you well.

2

Qualification

We review your application against real criteria: coachability, implementation capacity, foundation, and goal clarity.

3

Blueprint

Every applicant receives a Financial Blueprint Assessment — a complete diagnostic of your financial infrastructure.

4

Acceptance

You receive a readiness decision: accepted, accepted with conditions, waitlisted, referred elsewhere, or invited to reapply.

5

Education

You begin with the education mapped to your specific roadmap — not generic content.

6

Implementation

You do the work. CHEW provides strategy and education; you provide the follow-through.

7

Progress

Your action items and milestones live in your CHEW Command Center, tracked as you go.

8

Optimization

As your systems mature, we refine strategy based on what's actually working for you.

9

Capital Readiness

If you're pursuing funding, we help you prepare the documentation and positioning lenders and investors actually look for.

10

Long-Term Advisory

CHEW measures success in years, not a single transaction. Membership continues the relationship after your program.

Where to start

Every stage starts the same way.

There's one entry point to all ten stages: apply for admission. If you have questions before applying, reach out through our contact form or email leroyt@joinchew.com directly.

Start with your application.
