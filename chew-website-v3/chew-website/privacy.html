Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy has not yet been reviewed by legal counsel and should be treated as a working draft until that review is complete.

What we collect

When you use a form on this website, we collect the information you provide — name, email, phone number, and any message or document you choose to share. We do not sell your information.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to your inquiry, schedule and deliver consultations, and process payments through our payment processor, Stripe. We do not share your information with outside parties except as necessary to deliver our services or as required by law.

Payment information

CHEW does not directly store your card or bank information. Payments are processed securely by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor.

Document uploads

Any documents you upload as part of a consultation are stored securely and access is limited to the CHEW team members directly involved in your engagement.

Your choices

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting leroyt@joinchew.com.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as CHEW grows. Material changes will update the date above.
