FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is CHEW a credit repair company?

No. CHEW does not offer credit repair services, and we don't dispute accurate information on your credit report on your behalf. We focus on financial education and strategy, including helping you understand your own credit report and build genuine credit health over time.

Does CHEW guarantee loan approval or a specific credit score increase?

No, and we're deliberately built not to. Anyone guaranteeing a specific financial outcome isn't being straight with you. We focus on sound strategy and honest education instead.

Is CHEW a registered investment advisor, CPA firm, or law firm?

No. CHEW provides financial education and strategy guidance. For services that require a licensed investment advisor, CPA, or attorney, we'll tell you directly and help point you toward the right kind of professional.

How much does a consultation cost?

Pricing depends on your specific situation and the scope of support you need. See our Pricing page for how we structure engagements, or book a consultation for a direct quote.

Is my information secure?

Protecting client information is a foundational requirement for CHEW, not an afterthought. As our client portal and document upload systems go live, they'll be built to modern security and encryption standards from day one.

Does CHEW work with entrepreneurs as well as individuals?

Yes. We offer both individual financial strategy services and business-focused services for entrepreneurs, including funding readiness and cash flow management.

How do I get started?

The fastest way is to book a consultation, or email us directly at leroyt@joinchew.com if you'd like to ask a question first.
