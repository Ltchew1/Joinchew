About CHEW

Creating Honest Economic Wealth — starting with the name.

From the Founder

Why CHEW exists.

I started CHEW after seeing how much confusion and misinformation surrounds personal and business finance — and how often that confusion gets exploited. CHEW is built on a simple standard: explain things clearly, never promise what we can't control, and treat every client's long-term financial future like it matters more than a quick sale.

Leroy Thompson

Founder, CHEW LLC

Mission

Financial progress, built on honesty.

CHEW exists to help individuals and entrepreneurs improve their financial position through education, strategy, technology, and personalized guidance — delivered without the exaggerated promises that plague the financial services industry.

Vision

America's most trusted financial strategy platform.

We're building CHEW as a long-term institution, not a quick win. That means investing in real infrastructure, real compliance, and a brand voice that never overpromises — even when a bolder claim might sell faster.

Where we are today

Tested before it was public.

As we're able to share specific outcomes and client stories, this page will be the place we do it.
