3. Fees and Payment

3.1 Entry Fee. You pay the entry fee for the selected program at the time of signing this Agreement, as quoted at checkout.

3.2 Remainder Balance (Infrastructure and Executive only). The balance of the full program fee is due according to the payment option you select at checkout. Until it's paid in full, CHEW may limit delivery of program components that have not yet been rendered.

3.3 Membership Recurring Fee. Membership includes a 30-day trial period beginning today. Unless you cancel before the trial ends, CHEW will begin billing the recurring monthly membership fee automatically at the end of the trial period, and monthly thereafter, until cancelled.

3.4 Refunds.

  • The entry fee is refundable in full if you request cancellation in writing within 3 business days of payment, provided you have not yet attended a strategy session or received the Financial Blueprint Assessment.
  • Once a strategy session has been delivered or the Financial Blueprint Assessment has been provided, the entry fee is non-refundable.
  • Remainder balance payments are non-refundable once paid, except where required by applicable law.
  • Membership fees are non-refundable for any partial month; you may cancel at any time to stop future charges, per Section 5.

3.5 Late or Failed Payments. If a scheduled remainder or membership payment fails, CHEW will notify you and attempt to collect payment. CHEW may suspend portal access and further service delivery until payment is resolved. Continued non-payment beyond 15 days of notice is grounds for termination under Section 5.

4. Client Obligations and Program Participation

Your engagement with CHEW is a two-sided commitment. In exchange for the services described in Section 1, you agree to:

4.1 Honest Disclosure. Provide accurate and complete information about your financial situation, both during the application process and throughout the engagement.

4.2 Task Completion. Complete assigned action items within the timelines set by your strategist. Tasks are tracked in the Client Portal. A task you mark complete is reviewed and verified by CHEW — self-reported completion alone does not constitute program progress.

4.3 Session Attendance. Attend scheduled strategy sessions. You may reschedule any session with at least 24 hours' notice, at no penalty. A session missed without that notice, and not rescheduled within 7 days, is forfeited.

4.4 Responsiveness. Respond to strategist communications within a reasonable time. CHEW cannot deliver strategy or verify tasks for a client who cannot be reached.

4.5 Good-Faith Participation. Engage with the program honestly and in good faith.

4.6 Forfeiture of Program Access. Your spot in the program may be forfeited if: you miss two consecutively scheduled sessions without the notice described in 4.3; you are unresponsive to strategist communications for 14 consecutive days; you provide materially false information relevant to the strategy being built; or you engage in abusive or harassing conduct toward CHEW staff.

Before treating a forfeiture condition as triggered, CHEW will make a reasonable attempt to reach you and confirm the situation. If forfeiture is confirmed: your Client Portal access is revoked, no further sessions or deliverables are provided, amounts already paid are not refunded, and any remainder balance already invoiced under an installment plan remains due under the original payment schedule.

5. Term, Cancellation, and Termination

Infrastructure / Executive — You may cancel at any time by written notice. Refunds, if any, are governed by Section 3.4. Cancelling does not relieve you of a remainder balance already invoiced for services already delivered.

Membership — You may cancel at any time, effective at the end of the current billing period, through the Client Portal's billing management or by written notice. There is no early-termination fee.

CHEW may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if your conduct meets the forfeiture conditions in Section 4.6, or if a payment failure is not resolved as described in Section 3.5. On termination for any reason, your Client Portal access ends and CHEW's obligation to deliver further services ends.
